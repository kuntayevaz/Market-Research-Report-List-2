<p><p>Bi-oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) laminating film is a type of film made from polypropylene that has been stretched and oriented in both the machine and transverse directions. The increasing demand for flexible packaging solutions, particularly in the food and beverage industry, is driving the growth of the market. BOPP laminating film is commonly used in bubble mailers, envelope windows, and protective bags for online shipments.</p><p>The future outlook for the BOPP laminating film market is also promising.
